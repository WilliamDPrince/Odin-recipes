Creamy Gnocchi Pesto Recipe

Description

This creamy gnocchi is a simple, delicious Italian cuisine

Ingredients

Gnocchi Pasta

Pesto Sauce

Flour

Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

1. Bring one gallon of water to a boil. Add salt to taste.

2. Dump in Gnocchi Pasta. Boil until they rise to top of water.

3. Strain pasta and put aside

4. Add one cup of pasta water, half a cup of basil pesta, and half a cup of flour to a pan.

5. Stir to combine into a creamy sauce.

6. Stir in cooked pasta and add parmesan cheese.
